Our Heritage

The Bulldog Story

Pioneering premium boutique cannabis and luxury lifestyle lounge in the heart of Wellington. Housed in a historic landmark, we combine old-world hospitality with certified premium-level quality.

A Historic Sanctuary in 1 Rose Street

Housed inside the iconic Character Building at Unit 6, 1 Rose Street, Wellington, The Bulldog Dispensary stands as a physical testament to craft and community. We transformed a beautiful historic structure into Wellington's premier boutique dispensary.

Our store features high brickwork, secure off-street parking, and a relaxed, welcoming layout. Designed for high transparency, our patrons can browse profiles, review verified lab test documents, and receive personalized advice in a space where they feel safe, respected, and valued.

Did you know?

All of our organic flower strains are sourced exclusively from craft, single-origin boutique farms in the Western Cape. We run meticulous multi-tier quality checks to ensure no harmful pesticides, consistent cannabinoid levels, and rich terpene structures.
